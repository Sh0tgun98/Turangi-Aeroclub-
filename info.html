💳 Landing Fees & Payment

Turangi Aerodrome is operated and maintained by the Aero Club with minimal external support. Landing fees help maintain the runway, facilities, and ongoing improvements.

  • Private operators: $10 per landing
  • Commercial operators: $25 per landing
  • Invoiced after 7 days: Additional $20 per landing

⚠️ Notices & Operational Information

Always check NOTAMs and the AIP before operating into NZTN.
  • Uncontrolled aerodrome
  • Be mindful of local noise-sensitive areas
  • Watch for wildlife on and near the runway
  • Emergency and rescue helicopter operations may occur without notice
